# /deep-interview — Socratic Requirements Gathering

## Trigger
`/deep-interview <topic>`

## Agents
- **metis** (primary): Strategic questioning
- **scribe** (output): Document findings

## Flow
1. Ask 3-5 clarifying questions about the user's goal.
2. Synthesize answers into a requirements document.
3. Identify assumptions and validate with the user.
4. Produce a final spec that can feed into /plan or /team.

## Rules
- Maximum 3 rounds of questions.
- Each question must be specific and actionable.
- Summarize understanding after each round.
- Final output is a structured requirements document.
